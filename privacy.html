Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Privacy at a Glance

Your privacy is our priority. Vylo is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. All your data stays on your device. You don't need to create an account, and we don't track your affirmation usage.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vylo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application Vylo (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

By using Vylo, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Vylo is built on a local-first architecture, which means:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or log in to use Vylo.
  • No Affirmation Tracking: We do not track which affirmations you view, favorite, or interact with.
  • No User-Generated Content: Any personalization data you provide (such as your name during onboarding) is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or access your location information.
  • No Contact Information: We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal device data.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide personalized features, Vylo stores certain information directly on your device using iOS's local storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and App Groups). This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Onboarding Preferences: Your name (if provided), selected categories, notification preferences, theme choices, and app icon preference.
  • Usage Data: Your daily streak count, favorite affirmations, affirmation history, and onboarding completion status.
  • App Settings: Language preference, notification schedule, selected categories, and theme customization.
  • Widget Data: Affirmations displayed in your Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets are stored in a shared App Group container accessible only by Vylo and its widgets.

You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

While we don't collect your data, we use third-party services to provide essential functionality. These services may collect limited information as described below:

4.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifiers (generated by RevenueCat, not linked to your identity)
  • Purchase transaction information (subscription status, purchase dates)
  • Device information (iOS version, device type)

RevenueCat's Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.2 Mixpanel (Analytics)

We use Mixpanel to understand how users interact with the App and improve the user experience. Mixpanel may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (screens viewed, features used)
  • Device information (iOS version, device model, screen size)
  • App performance metrics (crashes, load times)

Mixpanel does not receive any personally identifiable information from us. You can opt out of analytics tracking in the App settings.

Mixpanel's Privacy Policy: https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy/

4.3 TikTok SDK (Attribution)

We use the TikTok SDK for attribution purposes to understand which marketing campaigns are effective. The TikTok SDK may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV, if you've granted permission)
  • App installation and first-launch events
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)

You can limit ad tracking through your iOS device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

TikTok's Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

4.4 Apple (Notifications & Widgets)

When you enable notifications, we use Apple's Push Notification Service (APNs) to schedule local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated and delivered locally on your device—we do not send push notifications from a server.

Widgets use iOS's WidgetKit framework and access affirmation data stored in a shared App Group container on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Vylo is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Security

Since all your personal data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong passcode or biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) on your device
  • Keeping your iOS software up to date
  • Only downloading Vylo from the official Apple App Store

For data transmitted to third-party services (subscriptions, analytics), we rely on industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information:

7.1 European Economic Area (EEA) - GDPR Rights

  • Right to Access: Since we don't store your data, there's nothing for us to provide. All your data is on your device.
  • Right to Deletion: Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of analytics tracking in the App settings.

7.2 California - CCPA Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (see Section 3)
  • Request deletion of personal information (uninstall the App)
  • Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell your information)

7.3 Other Jurisdictions

If you reside in a jurisdiction with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Contact us at privacy@vylo.app for assistance.

8. International Data Transfers

Since your data is stored locally on your device, there are no international transfers of your personal data. However, third-party services (RevenueCat, Mixpanel, TikTok SDK) may process data in countries outside your own. These services comply with applicable data protection frameworks such as GDPR.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the App or via email (if we have your contact information)
  • Post the updated policy on our website

Your continued use of Vylo after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vylo.app
  • Support: support@vylo.app

11. Data Protection Officer

For users in the European Economic Area, if you have questions about how we handle your data or wish to exercise your GDPR rights, you can contact our Data Protection Officer at:

dpo@vylo.app

12. Commitment to Privacy

At Vylo, we believe that your wellness journey is deeply personal. That's why we've built an app that respects your privacy from the ground up. We don't need your data to provide value—our mission is simply to help you cultivate self-love, confidence, and emotional wellness through daily affirmations.

Thank you for trusting Vylo with your daily self-care practice.
